Introduction


We reserve the facilities in our school library everyday. And our group finds that there can be some inprovement on the reservation systems. So this project is a basic library facilities reservation systems with some add-ons.

The basic functions include:

1:Guests can browse and search all available facilities and rooms.

2:Users can form a group by adding friends and delete a group.

3:Users can make a reservation on behalf of a group and every member of the group gains same permission.

4:Users can manage their own reservations on "My Account" page, i.e. canceling or altering.

5:Administrator can add, remove, manage status of facilities and users.

The inprovement functions include:

1:The system will cancel this reservation automatically if grace time expires(15 minutes after the reserved time). After that, the facility or the room becomes "first-come, first-serve".

2:Administrators can manage the availability of facilities and rooms and the status of users. If the availability is maintainting, no one can reserved it.

3:We simulate the process of gaining access for the facilities by swiping the card, or login their account on computer (for computer only).
